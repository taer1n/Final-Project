Photography

To me, photography is capturing the emotions and memories of a specific moment in time. Whether the moment is spending time with my family, eating lunch with friends or just pictures of myself, I am able to recall the exact experience of the moment, and remember the good memories associated with the photo. I am able to capture the diversity of cultures from the different places where I've traveled or lived. When you look at the pictures, you can remember the feelings, places, and people.
